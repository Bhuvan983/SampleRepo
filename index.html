About Us

We are a team of five dedicated third-year Computer Science and Engineering students from GITAM passionate about bridging the gap between academia and professional growth. At ScholarBridge, our mission is to facilitate meaningful connections between students, faculty, and alumni, fostering a collaborative environment for research and career development.
Our platform is designed to empower students by providing easy access to faculty for research collaboration and to alumni for career guidance and networking opportunities.

What We Offer

Bridging the gap between students, faculty, and alumni.

Faculty Connect

Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between students and faculty, facilitating meaningful research collaborations. Students can easily browse through faculty profiles, which highlight their research interests, current projects, and publications.

Alumni Connect

We help alumni stay connected with their alma mater and provide valuable guidance to current students. By connecting with alumni, students can gain valuable insights into various industries, learn from real-world experiences, and receive support as they transition from academic life to their professional careers.
